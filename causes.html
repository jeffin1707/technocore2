Causes of Asteroid Falls

Understand why asteroids fall towards Earth and what triggers their paths.

Solar System Dynamics

Gravitational pull from planets can alter asteroid orbits, sending them toward Earth.

Collisions

Asteroids can collide with other celestial objects, changing trajectory and speed.

Cometary Breakups

Fragments from comets may fall to Earth as meteoroids when their paths cross Earth's orbit.

Yarkovsky Effect

Small forces like sunlight heating an asteroid unevenly can change its orbit over time.

Gravitational Resonance

Asteroids in orbital resonance with planets can be nudged into Earth-crossing orbits.

Random Perturbations

Minor collisions or radiation forces can occasionally send asteroids off-course.
